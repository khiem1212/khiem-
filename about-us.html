about us

OUR STORY

MORE THAN 20 YEARS OF EXPERIENCE IN THE MANUFACTURING BUSINESS OF HARDWOOD FURNITURE

Dai Xuyen Co., Ltd. was founded in 2005. It starting from a small joinery workshop established by our founder – Mr. Pham Van Xuan.

From 2000 to 2015, we had more than 700 workers for a specific antique furniture line, with thousands of custom samples made yearly.

From this great success, Mr. Pham Van Xuan continued to invest in machinery and systems to meet the world wide range of furniture.

From 2015 until today, we started doing mass production of furniture with modern designs.

OUR FOUNDER

STRONG EXPERIENCE, PASSION AND CREATIVITY FOR MAKING FURNITURE

Mr. Xuan moved from the North to the South of Vietnam at the age of 15 and began pursuing his dream.

Through many years of dedication, he sharpened his skills and experience by working with different ranges and designs of furniture.

He and his team are well-known as artisans for restoring damaged furniture, and making antique furniture such as Louis chairs, moldings, doors, and so on for the Old French Castles.

It was his greatest base for making antique furniture.

OUR PEOPLE

EVERY INDIVIDUAL MATTERS

At Dai Xuyen Furniture, we are grounded in the very first principle: People Focus.

Every employees have their own strengths, from teams of manufacturing, technology to sales, customer support.

All voices are heard, so that all people can improve day by day as an individual and add more values to the company as a whole.

Dai Xuyen Furniture strictly follows the labor law, and says no to child labor.

We guarantee labor benefits and safety for all employees under Vietnam National Health Insurance. We make sure that our employees’ health conditions are checked up annually.
